# HOW TO CHOOSE SECTION

## ENGLISH (EN)

**Header:**
> Start here

**Question 1: What's this money for?**
> - Emergency fund → Safe Harbor or Stable Growth
> - Something in the next 2 years → Goal Keeper or Steady Progress
> - Something in 2-5 years → Patient Builder or Balanced Builder
> - Long-term wealth → Steady Compounder, Wealth Accelerator, Yield Maximizer, or Full Throttle

**Question 2: How do you feel about price swings?**
> - "I don't want any" → Stick to the Stable Returns column
> - "I understand them and can wait out dips" → Consider the Growth Potential column
> - "I'm not sure" → Start stable. Learn. You can always add growth exposure later.

**Question 3: What would you do if your balance dropped 20%?**
> - "I'd panic and withdraw" → Stable Returns strategies only
> - "I'd wait for it to recover" → Low-medium growth strategies
> - "I'd add more money" → You might be ready for higher growth exposure

**Golden Rule:**
> When in doubt, start safe. You can always move up later.
