## Pass-Through Fees (Not diBoaS Revenue)

Third-party fees (card processing, network gas, protocol fees) are passed through at cost with full transparency. These vary by payment method, blockchain, and asset.

## Legacy Fee References

Any document referencing the following is **outdated** and should be updated:
- 0.12% (old B2B receive fee)
- 0.09% or 0.9% (old invest/transfer fees)
- 0.75% (old off-ramp fee)
- 0.5% deposit, $1.99 withdrawal, $0.25 transfer (original docs/fees.md)
- 0.25% crypto trade
- Subscription tiers ($9.99, $19.99, $500/month)
- 0.39% buy/invest fee (Fee Lab v3.3 — changed to FREE in v3.4)

If you encounter these rates anywhere in the codebase, they are bugs.
