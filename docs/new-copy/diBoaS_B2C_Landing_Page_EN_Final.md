## Section 10: FAQ

**Section Header:** Questions? We've got answers.

**Q: Is diBoaS a bank?**
No. diBoaS is a non-custodial platform — we don't hold, access, or manage your money. You control your own funds directly through your personal wallet. We provide the tools — you make the decisions.

**Q: How is this possible without high fees?**
By removing the middlemen — the branches, the executives, the legacy systems. We use modern technology that costs a fraction of what traditional finance costs. We only charge when you sell assets (0.12%) or withdraw (0.75%).

**Q: Is my money safe?**
Your money is secured by you. We use industry-leading security providers, but ultimately you're in control. That's the point — no one else can access your funds without your permission. Important to know: crypto-assets are not covered by deposit guarantee schemes. The value of your assets can go up or down, and you could lose some or all of your investment.

**Q: What if I don't understand investing?**
That's exactly who we built this for. Our goal is to make it so simple that anyone can do it. No jargon. No complicated decisions. Just clear options and transparent information.

**Q: What's the minimum amount to start?**
Start with as little as $5 (US), €5 (EU), or R$10 (Brazil). No minimum balance requirements.
