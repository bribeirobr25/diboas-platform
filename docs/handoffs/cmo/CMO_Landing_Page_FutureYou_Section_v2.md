## Section Overview

The Future You Calculator is a compound growth visualization tool that allows anonymous visitors to see what monthly savings could become over time. It compares diBoaS strategy returns against traditional bank savings rates.

### Key Principles
- **Anonymous:** No account required
- **Educational:** Shows the gap, doesn't sell
- **Interactive:** Slider + time buttons for exploration
- **Compliant:** Includes projection disclaimer
- **Conservative:** Uses 8% (middle of Safe Harbor 6-10% range)

## Calculation Parameters

| Parameter | Value | Source |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| **Bank rate** | 0.5% annual | ECB average savings rate |
| **diBoaS rate** | 8% annual | Conservative estimate (Safe Harbor mid-range) |
| **Compounding** | Monthly | Standard DCA calculation |

### Formula

```
FV = P × ((1 + r/12)^(n×12) - 1) / (r/12)

Where:
- FV = Future Value
- P = Monthly payment
- r = Annual interest rate
- n = Number of years
```
