## Purpose
Help people understand something new in 60 seconds. Not persuade â€” educate.

## Voice
Teacher sharing a revelation. "Let me show you something interesting."

## Emotional Arc
1. **Screen 1 (Curiosity):** "Here's something you might not know"
2. **Screen 2 (Understanding):** "Here's why it matters"
3. **Screen 3 (Agency):** "You can try it"
4. **Screen 4 (Proof):** "See? It works"
5. **Screen 5 (Invitation):** "Want to learn more?"

## Design Principles
- Mobile-first (thumb-friendly CTAs)
- No signup required to start
- Educational, not salesy
- Shareable moment at Screen 4

# TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

## Animation Guidelines

| Screen | Animation Type | Duration | Notes |
|--------|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Information reveal | Subtle, sequential | Text appears in reading order |
| 2 | Comparison highlight | 0.5s | Numbers emphasized |
| 3 | Input focus | Immediate | Friendly, inviting |
| 4 | Counter increment | 3-5 seconds | Real-time feel |
| 4 | Share card slide | 0.3s | Smooth appearance |
| 5 | Warm welcome | 1s | Subtle, not over-celebratory |

## Share Card Generation

**Card Design Philosophy:**
- Educational framing ("Something I learned")
- Not bragging, sharing knowledge
- Clean, readable, shareable
