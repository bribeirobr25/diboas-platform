# PART 7: IMPORTANT NOTES

---

## Language Principles

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|----------------|
| "Sign up now" | "If it makes sense, sign when ready" |
| "Don't miss out" | "At least you understand the numbers now" |
| "Exclusive opportunity" | "Early access" |
| "Act fast" | "Take your time to decide" |
| "You need this" | "This might be interesting for you" |

---

## Consultative Approach

- Never pressure
- Always offer alternative if not a fit
- Acknowledge their decision is valid either way
- Focus on education, not persuasion
- Be honest about risks and limitations

---

## If They Say No

> "Appreciate you taking the time to learn about it. If anything changes, you know where to find me. At least you understand the treasury math now — that knowledge is useful regardless."

---

## Legal Notes

1. **Non-Binding:** LOI creates no legal obligation
2. **No Investment Advice:** diBoaS doesn't provide investment advice
3. **Variable Yields:** Rates are not guaranteed
4. **Risk Acknowledgment:** DeFi involves smart contract risk
5. **Regulatory Status:** Subject to pending authorization
